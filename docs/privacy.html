Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 14, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Memory Lane (the "Extension") collects, uses, and protects your personal data.

2. Data Collected

  • Notes & Snapshots: Text you voluntarily save via the extension popup. Stored locally in Chrome Sync.
  • URL Information: The web address of pages where you save notes, used solely to retrieve the correct note.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your saved notes when you revisit the corresponding page.
  • Synchronize notes across devices signed into the same Chrome account.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal data to third parties.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access & Portability: You can view and export your notes via Chrome's Sync settings.
  • Deletion: Remove notes individually through the extension or delete all data via Chrome's extension management page.
  • Rectification: Edit any saved note at any time.

6. Security

All data is stored using Chrome's encrypted Sync storage. No external servers are involved.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact

If you have questions, please contact us at chiragsinghal127@gmail.com.
